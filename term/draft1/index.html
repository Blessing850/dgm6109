Sleep Patterns and Sleep Quality

Name: Blessing Chizarum Amadi

Hypothesis:
As my daily hours of sleep and bedtime consistency increase, my sleep quality increases.

This visualization explores my sleep habits over 19 days. The scatterplot/bubbleplot shows how sleep duration and bedtime affect the quality of my sleep.

Each circle represents one day of recorded sleep data.

  • X-axis: Hours slept
  • Y-axis: Sleep quality (1–5)
  • Circle size: Minutes awake before falling asleep
  • Color: Bedtime category

From this visualization, I observed that days with earlier bedtimes and longer sleep generally resulted in higher sleep quality, which supports my hypothesis.
